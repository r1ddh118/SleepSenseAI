# SleepSense AI — IRB Submission Checklist

## Study: Prospective Validation of SleepSense AI vs In-Lab PSG

## Target N: 50 participants | Duration: 6 months

---

## Pre-Submission Requirements

- [ ] Principal Investigator identified and credentialed
- [ ] Co-investigator list with affiliations
- [ ] Study site confirmed (sleep clinic / hospital)
- [ ] Funding source declared

## Protocol Documents

- [ ] Study protocol v1.0 (see template below)
- [ ] Informed consent form (patient-facing, plain language)
- [ ] Participant information sheet
- [ ] Data management plan
- [ ] Adverse event reporting plan
- [ ] Privacy impact assessment (DISHA compliant for India)

## Inclusion / Exclusion Criteria

**Inclusion:**

- Adults 18–75 years
- Clinical suspicion of sleep-disordered breathing (Epworth > 10 or snoring complaint)
- Able to give written informed consent

**Exclusion:**

- Known severe cardiac arrhythmia (affects IBI/HR signals)
- Active skin conditions at E4 wrist placement site
- Pregnancy
- Inability to wear wristband for 8 hours

## Data Collection Protocol

- Night 1: In-lab PSG (gold standard)
- Night 2 or 3 (within 1 week): E4 wristband at home via SleepSense
- Primary outcome: Agreement between SleepSense binary label and PSG AHI ≥ 15 classification
- Secondary outcomes: Sensitivity, Specificity, Kappa, AUC-ROC

## Target Metrics for Publication

| Metric          | Minimum acceptable | Target   |
|-----------------|--------------------|----------|
| Sensitivity     | 0.75               | ≥ 0.85   |
| Specificity     | 0.70               | ≥ 0.80   |
| AUC-ROC         | 0.80               | ≥ 0.88   |
| Cohen's Kappa   | 0.60               | ≥ 0.70   |
